Operator Notes

Command Guide

Start

Click Start System, then allow camera and microphone access in your respected browser.

Domains

Peace Sign + "Domain Expansion" opens Unlimited Void.

Fist + "Domain Expansion" opens Shrine.

Techniques

Blue sign + "Blue" creates Blue.

Open palm + "Red" creates Red.

When Blue and Red are active, say "Purple" or "Hollow Purple".

Summon

Two fists + "With this treasure, I summon" activates Mahoraga.

The hidden direct summon button remains available in code for demos.
